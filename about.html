Mission

Helping men grow deeper in their relationship with Jesus Christ.

Man Camp South exists to disciple men into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ through fellowship, biblical teachings, and intentional community. Every part of the weekend is meant to create space for men to step away from routine, hear truth clearly, and build stronger bonds with other believers.

The weekend is designed to be meaningful rather than crowded or flashy, with room for teaching, solitude, shared meals, worship, conversation, and practical next steps in faith.

A

Fellowship

Meaningful connection with other men in a setting that encourages honesty, support, encouragement, and Christ-centered brotherhood.

B

Biblical teaching

Teaching, worship, and service moments designed to keep the weekend centered on Jesus Christ and faithful spiritual growth.

C

Intentional community

An outdoors-forward weekend with room for reflection, structure, shared experience, and a practical next step toward deeper faith.

Guest speaker

Jonnie W.

Jonnie W. blends music, off-beat standup, and a clean, family-friendly live comedy style. His bio notes performances in 48 states, regular SiriusXM play, and a Dry Bar Comedy special with more than 5 million views.

He is known for mixing stand-up, storytelling, and music into a set that is memorable, approachable, and a strong fit for a weekend built around encouragement, fellowship, and faith.
